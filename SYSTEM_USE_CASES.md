## System Use Cases by Role

This section translates the full system capabilities into human-readable scenarios that can be reused in proposals and stakeholder documentation. Each role is described in terms of the business outcomes and workflows they can accomplish, without referencing technical implementation details.

---

### Guests & Public Visitors
- Explore salon services, pricing, and promotions before registering.
- Review staff profiles and customer feedback to build trust.
- Check service availability and initiate a booking request in minutes.
- Complete secure online payments and receive confirmation with a booking reference.
- View operating hours, contact details, and other salon information while evaluating the business.

---

### Registered Customers
- Access a personalized dashboard that highlights upcoming appointments, rewards, and wallet balances.
- Book new services, reschedule existing appointments, or cancel when necessary—always with clear policy guidance.
- Browse a multi-salon marketplace, shop salon products, and save preferred services to a favorites list.
- Use smart search across services, salons, and staff members to discover the best match for their needs.
- Manage a digital wallet: add funds, view transactions, and use stored payment methods for quicker checkout.
- Track product orders and appointment history, download receipts, and view detailed payment breakdowns.
- Participate in loyalty and referral programs, redeem rewards, and monitor progress toward incentives.
- Leave verified reviews for completed appointments to share feedback with the salon community.

---

### Salon Staff
- View a live schedule of all appointments assigned to them for the day and upcoming weeks.
- Update appointment statuses (confirmed, in progress, completed, etc.) and add service notes after each visit.
- Review detailed client profiles—including contact information and previous notes—to prepare before sessions.
- Confirm walk-in or cash payments when authorized, ensuring timely updates to the payment ledger.
- Request personal leave, check approval statuses, and view any appointments affected by their absence.
- Receive centralized notifications about schedule changes, reassigned bookings, or urgent updates.
- Consult the AI assistant for step-by-step service guidance, policy reminders, or escalation procedures.

---

### Receptionists & Front Desk Teams
- Manage the master appointment calendar, balancing staff availability with customer requests in real time.
- Create bookings on behalf of customers (in-person or telephonic), assign staff, and capture special requests.
- Quickly search for clients, services, or appointments to answer inquiries, make adjustments, or upsell add-on services.
- Monitor the day’s schedule for conflicts, late arrivals, or gaps and take proactive action.
- Stay informed through notifications about payment confirmations, cancellations, or staff changes.
- Lean on the chatbot for quick policy checks, pricing confirmations, or troubleshooting tips while assisting customers.

---

### Managers
- Maintain the service catalog: update descriptions, pricing, durations, and promotional offers.
- Analyze performance using financial, appointment, client, and staff productivity reports to spot trends.
- Monitor payments across methods, track outstanding balances, trigger refunds, and approve cash settlements.
- Oversee inventory levels, suppliers, stock adjustments, and product sales performance.
- Review and action staff leave requests, ensuring adequate coverage through reassignment tools.
- Manage client relationships with an up-to-date directory, appointment history, and exportable data sets.
- Access a high-level user directory to confirm active roles, permissions, and account status.
- Utilize advanced search and filters to locate staff, services, or clients when resolving escalations.
- Collaborate with the AI assistant for data-driven recommendations, best practices, and operational checklists.

---

### Owners & Administrators
- Configure the entire tenant profile: salon name, branding, business description, tax and license details, and operating hours.
- Control payment infrastructure by adding or updating accepted payment methods and activating integrations.
- Recruit, onboard, and manage staff members and contractors, including role assignments and access levels.
- Approve or reject staff leave, reassign impacted appointments, and monitor customer notifications during disruptions.
- Launch and monitor promotional campaigns, ensuring services reflect time-limited offers and pricing strategies.
- Access comprehensive dashboards that summarize revenue, utilization, customer satisfaction, and operational health.
- Search across historical records, clients, and financial data to support audits or strategic planning.
- Receive policy alerts and compliance notifications to keep the salon aligned with regulations.
- Engage the AI assistant for executive summaries, what-if scenarios, and training resources for new initiatives.

---

### IT Support Personnel
- Assist owners and administrators in maintaining accurate business profiles and operating settings.
- Troubleshoot payment configuration, third-party integrations, and technical settings inside the system.
- Provide support during onboarding or when the salon introduces new digital processes or hardware.
- Diagnose issues faster with access to system-wide notifications, activity logs, and chat-assisted diagnostics.

---

### Super Administrators (Platform Operators)
- Review and approve new salon providers joining the platform, including document verification.
- Suspend, reactivate, or update tenant accounts in response to compliance or performance events.
- Access system-wide analytics dashboards to monitor platform growth, revenue, and feature adoption.
- Adjust platform-level settings and policies to ensure consistency across all participating salons.
- Rely on global search to pinpoint tenant records or support tickets for rapid intervention.
- Consult the AI assistant for platform announcements, release notes, and consolidated performance insights.

---

### Intelligent Discovery & Assistance (Shared Capability)
- Global, permission-aware search helps every role locate services, clients, staff, or financial records within seconds.
- The conversational AI assistant provides real-time guidance, answers policy questions, and recommends next steps tailored to the user’s context.
- Notification feeds deliver timely alerts about payments, schedule changes, approvals, and system announcements.
- Embedded help content and walkthroughs ensure rapid onboarding for new team members.

---

### Platform Services Available to All Authenticated Roles
- Sign in securely, update profile information, and manage personal credentials.
- Review personal activity history through audit-aware timelines that surface key actions.
- Participate in referral or rewards programs when eligible, ensuring engagement across roles.
- Experience consistent performance thanks to background scheduling, caching, and automated housekeeping services.

---

### Governance & Access Control Summary
- Navigation menus show only the options that align with each user’s role and permissions, preventing confusion.
- Page-level guards ensure that even direct URL access respects the same authorization rules as the UI.
- Guest accounts are automatically redirected away from protected areas, keeping confidential data secure.
